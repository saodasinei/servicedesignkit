About This Project

ServiceDesignKIT provides a comprehensive overview on existing design techniques for digital services and supports their selection and application. ServiceDesignKIT is provided by the research group "Information Systems & Service Design" (Prof. Dr. Alexander Maedche) of the Institute of Information Systems and Marketing (IISM) and Karlsruhe Service Research Institute (KSRI) at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Germany.
